# Start Here

This folder is the entry point for the AI learning workspace.

Use it to understand the learning strategy, the roadmap, and the order of study from beginner to advanced AI engineering.

## Files

- [START-HERE.md](START-HERE.md): first instructions, daily study loop, and first 30/60/90 day focus.
- [ROADMAP.md](ROADMAP.md): phased roadmap from AI fundamentals to senior AI engineer readiness.
- [2026-ai-roadmap.md](2026-ai-roadmap.md): end-to-end AI upskilling roadmap for agent and project reference.

## Outcome

After reading this folder, you should know:

- What to study first.
- What hands-on evidence to produce.
- How to connect AI learning to DevOps, SRE, and cloud engineering.
- How to turn learning into portfolio projects and resume bullets.
