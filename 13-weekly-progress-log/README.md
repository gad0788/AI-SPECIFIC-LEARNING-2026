# Weekly Progress Log

This folder tracks your AI learning progress week by week.

Use it to stay consistent and to convert learning into evidence for your resume.

## Files

- [progress-log.md](progress-log.md): weekly checklist and evidence tracker.

## What to Record

- What you studied.
- What you built.
- What failed or confused you.
- What tools you used.
- What evidence you produced.
- Resume bullet candidates.

## Weekly Review Questions

- What concept can I now explain clearly?
- What did I build or improve?
- What can I show in GitHub?
- What skill is now resume-ready?
- What should I study next?
