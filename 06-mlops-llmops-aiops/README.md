# MLOps, LLMOps, and AIOps

## MLOps

MLOps is the practice of building, deploying, monitoring, and maintaining ML models in production.

Topics:

- Datasets and feature stores.
- Training pipelines.
- Model registry.
- Deployment and serving.
- Drift monitoring.
- Model rollback.

## LLMOps

LLMOps applies production engineering to LLM applications.

Topics:

- Prompt versioning.
- Eval datasets.
- RAG evaluation.
- Model routing and fallback.
- Token/cost tracking.
- Latency monitoring.
- Guardrails.
- Human feedback loops.
- Provider reliability and rate limits.

## AIOps

AIOps applies AI to IT operations.

Use cases:

- Incident summarization.
- Log anomaly detection.
- Alert correlation.
- Runbook recommendation.
- Change-risk analysis.
- Root cause assistance.

## DevOps/SRE Learning Focus

For your background, prioritize LLMOps and AIOps before deep model training. You will get more career leverage by proving you can operate AI systems reliably.
